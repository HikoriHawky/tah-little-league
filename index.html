I'm putting you (yes, you!) through Competitive Pokémon Hell so you can suffer like I do and so I can introduce why I (Everii) love this godforsaken series.

For a prize!

Starting on January 21st, 2022, brackets will be set and run by yours truly. These matches do not have to be supervised in live time by me (Though if you want me to, I can), as Pokémon Showdown has a feature in which replays can be saved via a link and shown to others.

This announcement is to poll interest, and I will offer free coaching to those interested in Team Building, Type Matchups, Battle Knowledge/Logic, and important techniques, as to make this as fair to everyone as I can.

This Tournament will follow standard Smogon OU Rules:

  • https://www.smogon.com/dex/ss/formats/ou/
  • https://www.smogon.com/ss/articles/clauses

This includes Bans listed on the page, as well as some others induced by me.

You are allowed ONE Legendary/Mythical/Ultra Beast per team, and you are allowed to have two teams comprised of the same Pokémon. If your team is Barraskewda, Bisharp, Blacephelon, Blissey, Clefable, and Corviknight, you may have a secondary team of these same Pokémon, but with a different set of moves/items/abilities/etc, and you can switch freely between these two until your Set is over.

Bracket matches will be Best of 5, and there will be both a Loser's and Winner's bracket, seeding will be based on Random Number Generation
